Project Case Study

The Problem

FC Balokole, a local football club in my hometown, needed an online presence to engage with fans, share match updates, and manage team information. They lacked a professional website to showcase their activities and connect with the community.

The Solution

I developed a comprehensive website featuring team information, match schedules, player profiles, and community engagement tools. The site includes an interactive team gallery, live match fixtures, and a complete management system to help the club maintain their online presence.

Technologies Used
  • HTML5 for semantic markup and structure
  • CSS3 for responsive styling and modern design
  • JavaScript for interactive elements and dynamic content
  • Responsive Design principles for cross-device compatibility
Results

The website successfully launched with 15+ pages, achieving 100% mobile responsiveness and fast loading performance. The club now has a professional online presence to engage with fans and share updates about their activities.
